Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the urban landscape, Rajprabha Associates has been at the forefront of real estate development for over two decades. We have built our reputation on the twin pillars of quality and trust.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into one of the region's most respected real estate developers, with a portfolio spanning residential apartments, luxury villas, and commercial complexes.

Every project we undertake reflects our core commitment: to deliver not just a property, but a home where families can build their futures.

  • 20+ years of experience in real estate development
  • 150+ completed projects across the region
  • 5000+ satisfied homeowners
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified construction processes
  • Award-winning architectural designs

About Sainath Foundations

Sainath Foundations is the parent organization behind Rajprabha Associates, with a rich legacy spanning multiple decades in the construction and real estate industry. The group has been instrumental in shaping the skylines of several cities with landmark projects.

With a philosophy rooted in service, quality, and community development, Sainath Foundations has expanded its presence across residential, commercial, and infrastructure segments. The group's commitment to ethical business practices has earned it the trust of thousands of customers and partners.

Rajprabha Associates operates under the Sainath Foundations umbrella, inheriting its values of excellence, transparency, and customer-first approach. Together, they continue to build landmark spaces that stand the test of time.
